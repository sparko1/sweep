Safety

OFTEC, the body concerned with maintaining standards in the heating industry in the UK and republic of Ireland, recommends that oil fired boilers should be serviced once per year. This reduces the chance of carbon monoxide formation, and ensures that the system is running economically. With respect to chimney cleaning, it is recommended that, if you are burning a solid fuel fire on a daily basis, you should have your chimney swept twice per year.

About Us

Based in Belfast, Village Sweep is a locally owned chimney and oil boiler maintenance business. We offer a range of services, from standard chimney sweeping to oil boiler repairs and maintenance. We have recently begun offering flue lining and stove fitting services. Our employees are fully trained, OFTEC qualified tradesmen.

Our Philosophy

We aim to provide a professional service, both in terms of workmanship and approach. Our employees are fully trained in boiler servicing, flue lining and stove fitting. We take the safety of our customers extremely seriously, we test carefully for the presence of noxious gases and ensure that boilers are functioning to UK standards. We also make certain, that the job site is left clean and tidy.
